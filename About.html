Our Goal

Our goal is to engage and educate our users on the workings of a CANDU Calandria reactor. The CANDU virtual reality project is an educational resource for individuals, universities, colleges, and employers in the nuclear industry to use as a learning and training environment. We hope to modernize the education and training process regarding CANDU reactors.
