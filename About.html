About CANDU VR Experience

Learn about our mission to modernize nuclear education through immersive virtual reality technology.

Our Goal

Our goal is to engage and educate our users on the workings of a CANDU Calandria reactor. The CANDU virtual reality project is an educational resource for individuals, universities, colleges, and employers in the nuclear industry to use as a learning and training environment.

We hope to modernize the education and training process regarding CANDU reactors by leveraging cutting-edge VR technology to create immersive, interactive learning experiences that make complex nuclear science concepts accessible and engaging for learners of all levels.
