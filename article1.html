How to Access the CANDU Virtual Reality Experience

To access the CANDU Virtual Reality Educational Experience, click on the get started button on the homepage. A dropdown menu will appear asking if you are a student, parent, or educator. Choose the option that best describes you. A executable file will then automatically download, click on it to open it up. A popup window stating "windows protected your PC" will appear, simply click the "more info" text, and then the run anyways option. Another popup will appear asking if you give permission to allow this program to make changes to your PC, click the yes option. The installation setup will then appear, simply click the next, and install buttons and wait for the program to install. Once done make sure the launch option is checked, and click finish. The CANDU Virtual Reality Educational program is now installed on your computer and will launch. These steps are showcased in the video below.
