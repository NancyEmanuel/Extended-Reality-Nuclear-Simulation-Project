Navigate in the Candu Virtual Reality Experience

This video explains how to navigate in the CANDU Virtual Reality Educational experience fuel channel tutorial room upon entering. You will navigate by using the WASD Keys and your mouse or touchpad if you are not using a virtual reality headset. Press W to move forward, A and D to move left and right, and S to move backwards. Move your cursor around to look around the room. To pick up objects, click and hold on the left or right key. To teleport, you can click and hold the T key and aim at the teleportation targets located on the ground in each room.

This video explains how to navigate in the CANDU Calandria viewer room. You will navigate the same way as in the fuel channel room, by using the WASD Keys and your mouse or touchpad. Press W to slowly walk towards the full scale calandria, A and D to move left and right, and S to move backwards. You may want to experiment with moving backwards to get a view of the outer components of the calandria, and forwards to view the inner components such as the moderator. Move your cursor around to look around the room. To activate the color coding feature, use your cursor to touch on the different calandria components.
