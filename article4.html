How to Make The Most Out of Your Learning Experience

In order to make the most out of your learning experience, it is important to first spend some time reading the articles and watching the videos on the CANDU website. To get started, read the instruction articles on "How to access the CANDU Virtual Reality Educational Experience", and "How to navigate in the Candu Virtual Reality Educational Experience". Reading these articles will inform you on how to succesfully install, access, navigate, and interact with the CANDU Virtual Reality Educational Experience software. It is also useful to watch the 2 videos on the CANDU website homepage. The "Candu Calandria Reactor" video will give you the knowledge required to understand the components of CANDU reactors and sucessfully complete the CANDU quiz. Once you install and run the CANDU Virtual Reality Educational Experience program, make sure to listen carefully to the voice instructions and read the dialog so you know what tasks you should attempt. To escape from any virtual room you are in and return to the menu option, simply press the escape(esc) and shift buttons on your keyboard.
