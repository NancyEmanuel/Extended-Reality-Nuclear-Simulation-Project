Learn About VR Education

Virtual Reality is the computer generated simulated experience of a dimensional environment with visual and environmental stimuli. Virtual Reality isolates the user from the real world to create an immersion effect that gives the user the feeling of being present in a virtual world.

This video explains what Virtual Reality is and how it is being used to modernize the education and training process regarding CANDU reactors. The video goes into depth and explains what Virtual Reality is and how it can benefit students looking to learn more about CANDU Reactors.

VR education offers unprecedented opportunities for immersive learning, allowing students to explore complex nuclear systems in a safe, controlled environment. This technology bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical understanding, making complex concepts more accessible and engaging.
